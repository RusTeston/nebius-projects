Company

Nebius is a full-stack AI cloud company headquartered in Amsterdam, spun out of Yandex's AI infrastructure team. Led by founder Arkady Volozh, the company operates 400+ engineers and targets 1+ GW of secured power by end of 2026. Sister companies include Avride (autonomous vehicles), TripleTen (edtech), and stakes in Toloka and ClickHouse.

Core Products

AI Cloud — GPU clusters (GB300 to H100), InfiniBand networking, managed K8s & Slurm, 1 TB/s storage, self-healing infrastructure
Token Factory — 60+ open-source models, sub-second latency, 100M+ tokens/min, OpenAI-compatible API, zero-retention mode
Solutions — Data prep, model training, inference, fine-tuning, agentic search (Tavily), RAG, GenAI services
