Discovery Guides

The TRAIN Discovery Framework

Use this structured framework on every first discovery call. Each letter covers a critical dimension of the prospect's AI infrastructure situation.

T
Training & Inference Workloads
  • "Walk me through your current AI infrastructure. Are you pre-training, fine-tuning, or serving models in production?"
  • "How large are your largest training runs? Are you hitting GPU count or compute budget ceilings?"
  • "What's your current average GPU utilization? Are you seeing Model FLOPS Utilization below expectations?"
  • "Do you serve models in production today? What does your inference stack look like?"
R
Resources & Costs
  • "What's your current monthly cloud infrastructure spend?"
  • "How are you provisioning GPUs — on-demand, reserved, or spot?"
  • "How much engineering time does your team spend on infrastructure vs. model development?"
  • "Have you done any TCO analysis comparing providers? What did you find?"
A
Access & Agility
  • "How long does it take to provision a new GPU cluster today?"
  • "Have you ever had training jobs fail due to infrastructure issues? How did that impact your timeline?"
  • "Do you need the ability to scale up on short notice — for a large batch training run?"
I
Infrastructure Constraints & Compliance
  • "Do you have data residency requirements — European, US, or specific country?"
  • "Are you subject to compliance frameworks — HIPAA, SOC 2, GDPR, ISO 27001?"
  • "Is there a vendor approval process or security review for new cloud providers?"
N
Needs, Timeline & Next Steps
  • "What's driving this evaluation now — specific project, product launch, budget cycle?"
  • "If you could change one thing about your current infrastructure, what would it be?"
  • "Who else is involved in this decision — procurement, security, engineering leadership?"
  • "What would a successful outcome look like at the end of a 90-day trial?"
Pain Discovery Probes by Current Provider
If on AWS / Azure / GCP
  • How long did your last GPU quota approval take?
  • Have you had a critical training run delayed by capacity unavailability?
  • Have you modeled the cost delta vs. specialized AI clouds?
If on CoreWeave
  • Are you locked into long-term take-or-pay contracts?
  • Do you have GDPR or EU data residency requirements?
  • What managed services do you need beyond raw compute?
If on Lambda / RunPod
  • As you move to production, what gaps are you hitting?
  • Do you have enterprise SLA requirements your provider can't meet?
  • How much time does your team spend on Kubernetes management?

Objection-Handling Guides

❓"We're already on AWS/Azure/GCP and it's too hard to switch."
AcknowledgeSwitching cloud providers is a real concern — migration risk, retraining teams, and re-architecting can be significant.
ReframeFor a team spending $500K/month on AWS GPU, the Nebius cost difference is $3–4M per year in avoidable costs. And we're not asking you to migrate everything at once. Start with new AI projects.
ProofOur infrastructure uses standard Kubernetes and Slurm — your engineers don't need to learn new tools. Zero retraining required.
Close"Would it make sense to run a parallel pilot on one upcoming training run and compare cost and performance yourself?"
❓"We've never heard of Nebius — how do I know you'll be around in 3 years?"
AcknowledgeBrand trust is absolutely a fair consideration when choosing infrastructure that your AI products will depend on.
ProofNVIDIA invested $2 billion directly in Nebius. Meta signed a $27B contract. Microsoft signed $19B+. We have nearly $50B in contracted backlog and a $41B market cap with 27 analyst buy ratings. The largest GPU maker on Earth bet $2B on us.
ContextOur founding team built Yandex — one of Europe's largest internet companies — and brought hundreds of experienced infrastructure engineers with them. This is not a scrappy startup.
Close"Would a technical and financial due diligence session with our team give you the confidence to move to a pilot?"
❓"CoreWeave is the established leader — why should I go with you?"
AcknowledgeCoreWeave is a strong provider, especially for frontier AI labs doing massive training at scale.
DifferentiateBut CoreWeave is optimized for one thing: raw training scale. If you need managed Kubernetes, an inference API, EU data residency, HIPAA compliance, or self-serve access without a lengthy enterprise form — Nebius has all of that. And our H100 is $2.10/hr vs. CoreWeave's $4.76/hr.
ProofCoreWeave's revenue is 62% one customer. Ours is diversified across thousands — from Brave Search to JetBrains to regulated healthcare companies.
Close"What's most important: maximum raw GPU scale for frontier training, or a full-stack platform with better economics and compliance? That'll tell us who's the right fit."
❓"Your pricing seems too cheap — what's the catch?"
ExplainNebius achieves lower pricing through vertical integration — we design our own servers, racks, and data centers, eliminating the general-purpose overhead tax. SemiAnalysis independently modeled real workloads and confirmed Nebius has the lowest TCO.
ProofBrave Search runs 11 million AI-powered queries daily on us at nearly 100% GPU utilization. That's not the behavior of a customer worried about reliability.
Close"The best way to prove it is a pilot. Run one of your real workloads with trial credits and compare performance and cost yourself."
❓"We need to stay in the US — we can't use European infrastructure."
CorrectWe have US infrastructure — us-central1 is live today. We're building 300MW in Birmingham, Alabama and a 1,200MW campus in Independence, Missouri. The US is a core infrastructure priority.
Flip ItFor customers with EU users or GDPR requirements, our Finland data center is a unique differentiator — something CoreWeave and most US-only providers can't offer.
❓"We're under a long-term contract with our current provider."
AcknowledgeThat's a real constraint — we're not going to ask you to break contracts.
BridgeContracts end and budgets get reviewed. The best time to evaluate alternatives is before the renewal pressure hits. Starting a smaller pilot on Nebius today gives you the data and leverage you need for your next negotiation.
Close"When does your current contract come up for renewal?"
