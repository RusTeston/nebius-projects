01 — Partner Maturity Lifecycle · Four stages, each with a GTM unlock
Partner progression is earned, not assumed — each stage defines what a partner can do, not just what they know. Enablement exists to accelerate time-through-stage, not time-in-stage.
Stage 01
Onboarded · 100
Partner is registered, credentialed, and understands the Nebius ecosystem
What they can do Explain Nebius's product portfolio and partner program to a prospect
Enablement focus Platform orientation, Token Factory API quickstart, partner portal access
Unlock criteria Product knowledge assessment + live API integration demo passed
GTM Unlock
First qualified opportunity submitted to Nebius Partner Manager
Stage 02
Competent · 200
Partner can build and deliver Nebius-based solutions independently
What they can do Deploy a customer solution on Nebius AI Cloud without Nebius SA involvement
Enablement focus End-to-end solution lab, support escalation playbook, GTM alignment workshop
Unlock criteria Joint GTM plan submitted + solution architecture validated by Nebius SA
GTM Unlock
First jointly registered deal in active pipeline with a close date
Stage 03
GTM-Ready · 300
Partner is actively co-selling and generating Nebius-sourced pipeline
What they can do Run a full co-sell motion, close a partner-sourced deal, and expand the account
Enablement focus Advanced solutions (RAG, Agentic AI), expansion selling, joint case study development
Unlock criteria First customer win presented to Nebius Partner Manager with a documented replicable play
GTM Unlock
Joint demand generation campaign launched; joint case study in review
Stage 04
Expert · 400
Partner drives pipeline independently and expands the Nebius ecosystem
What they can do Independently source, win, deliver, and expand Nebius accounts without Nebius support
Enablement focus Thought leadership, Nebius Academy content contribution, sub-partner enablement
Unlock criteria Published joint case study + 6-month pipeline commitment with measurable milestones
GTM Unlock
Certified Nebius Partner — advanced co-sell resources + revenue sharing unlocked
02 — Enablement by Partner Type · Different partners need meaningfully different programs
Enablement dimension
● VAR Value-Added Reseller Sells Nebius as part of a broader solution. Revenue motion: resell + margin. Primary skill gap: commercial fluency and positioning.
● ISV / MSP Managed Service / Software Provider Builds on or manages Nebius for customers. Revenue motion: services + recurring. Primary skill gap: technical depth and independence.
● Alliance Alliance / Technology Partner Integrates their product with Nebius. Revenue motion: co-sell + joint GTM. Primary skill gap: GTM alignment and joint pipeline.

04 — Measuring Independence · GTM outcomes, not just completion rates
Time to first qualified opportunity < 30 days
Measured from partner portal activation to first deal registration — leading ramp indicator
Partner-sourced pipeline (90-day) vs target
Tracked against 90-day GTM plan commitment submitted at Stage 2 unlock
SA escalations per partner deal ↓ over time
Decreasing SA escalation rate signals growing technical independence — key independence metric
Certification gate pass rate > 80%
First-attempt pass rate across all three certification gates per cohort
Time to Expert stage < 10 wks
Full lifecycle from Onboarded through Expert certification — program efficiency metric
Joint case studies published per quarter
